PubMatic introduced Decision Fabric, a real-time intelligence layer that lets partners deploy proprietary decisioning models directly within the auction environment, where partners bring the model and PubMatic provides the data, infrastructure, and inventory scale. Partners can train models on supply-side bidstream data, deploy them as Docker containers, and activate audience qualification in under ten milliseconds.
