PubMatic held its 12th internal hackathon, its largest ever, with 111 teams and 416 participants across more than 5 countries over 48 hours. The event focused on agentic AI systems capable of planning and autonomous execution, with 61 teams (54%) being cross-functional and 14 made up entirely of non-engineers.
