The article examines how mobile game publishers should expand beyond cost-per-install (CPI) models to capture e-commerce and performance advertising budgets, noting mobile commerce is projected to surpass 50% of U.S. online retail sales by 2027. Publishers who diversify demand sources, control creative experiences, and leverage AI-driven optimization unlock incremental revenue without disrupting existing monetization stacks.
